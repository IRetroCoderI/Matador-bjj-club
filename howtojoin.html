How to Join

We are so happy to hear you are interested in joining our club and training the gentle art of Jiu-Jitsu! We get a lot of questions from students asking us how to join, so we decided to make a website!

Please follow all directions below.

All Players:

  • Registration Forms on Risk X
    • Waiver of Liability
    • Emergency Contact Card
    • Code of Conduct and Academic Release
    • Sports Clubs PREP - completed once in college career

New Players

  1. Complete New Participant Medical Forms and turn into the AT Office
  2. Schedule Baseline Concussion Test with Athletic Training (required for this club)
  3. Physical / EKG (required for this club)
  4. Sports Clubs 101: New participant Orientation in the Fall semester, Make-Up sessions will be held as needed.

Returning Players

  1. Complete Returning Participant Medical Forms and turn into the AT Office
  2. Submit a copy of a Government Issued ID (Driver's License, Passport, etc.) to the Sports Clubs Office

Important Deadlines

If the following is not completed by the listed dates, you will be unable to participate and join our club.
